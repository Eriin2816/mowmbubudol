Data Deletion Instructions

Effective date: September 7, 2025

Mowmbubudol respects your privacy and gives you control over your data. If you wish to request deletion of your personal data (including any data obtained through Meta products such as Facebook or Instagram), please follow the instructions below:

How to request deletion

  • Email: Send an email to marcelo.taweng@gmail.com with the subject line “Data Deletion Request” and include your account ID, username, or any details that help us identify your account.
  • Through Meta: If supported, you may also request deletion directly through Meta’s interface. This may call our Data Deletion Callback to process the request automatically.

What happens next

Upon receiving a valid request, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (for example, legal obligations or dispute resolution).

If you have questions about this process, please contact us at marcelo.taweng@gmail.com.

This page is provided to comply with Meta’s requirement for a publicly accessible Data Deletion Request URL.
